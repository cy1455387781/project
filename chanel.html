About Chanel, History and Headquarters Information History Chanel was founded in the year 1909. The company has been operational for 110 years now. The founder of the company was Coco Chanel. The founder of the company was responsible for making the ‘black’ colour famous and fashionable among women. Coco Chanel was also responsible for making the term ‘Little Black Dress’ or LBD famous. In the year 1921, the company launched the first perfume and is currently one of the best perfumes in the world. Even during World War II, the company was allowed to perfumes. After WWII, the founder of the company retired to Switzerland. The company then partnered with a France designer, known as Christian Dior, in order to design and manufacture jewellery and handbags. In the year 1971, the founder of the company passed away. After the founder's passing away, the company still managed to rake in huge profits and revenues, launched new products including perfumes as well - one of them known as Coco. In the year 2002, the company launched more new fragrances, known as Paraffection and Chance. As of the year 2018, the company is planning to move its headquarters to London. Headquarters Info The headquarters of the company is based in 9 West 57th Street, 44th Floor. The name of the city is New York, while the name of the state is New York, USA. The pin code of the area is 10019. About Chanel is a company based in France that focuses on the designing and manufacturing of various types of fashion apparels and also accessories as well. The company currently has its presence over 310 locations worldwide. The current CEO of the company is Nick Hochland. As of the year 2017, the total amount of revenues generated by the company is more than $9.6 billion. Also, as of the year 2018, the number of employees working for the company is more than 20,000. Services The primary services provided by the company include allowing customers to have a retail presence where various types of fashion apparels and accessories can be purchased. This includes eyewear, numerous beauty products, jewellery, perfumes, fragrances and finally, watches as well.
